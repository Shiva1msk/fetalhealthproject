Parameter Information

Prolonged Decelerations:

Measures heart rate drops during uterine contractions. Lower values are better.

Abnormal Short Term Variability:

Most important parameter. Extreme values (very low or very high) indicate problems.

Long Term Variability:

Percentage of time with abnormal patterns. High values are concerning.

Histogram Parameters:

Statistical measures of heart rate distribution. Normal range around 140-160 bpm.

Accelerations:

Heart rate increases indicating healthy fetal responses. Higher values are better.
