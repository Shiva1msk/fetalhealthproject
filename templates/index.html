Medical Parameters

The system analyzes 8 key fetal health indicators:

Prolonged Decelerations Heart rate drops during contractions
Short Term Variability Beat-to-beat heart rate changes
Long Term Variability Longer-term heart rate patterns
Histogram Analysis Statistical analysis of heart rate distribution

Health Classifications

NORMAL

Healthy fetal condition with no immediate concerns. Regular monitoring is sufficient.

SUSPECT

Requires medical attention and close monitoring. Clinical evaluation recommended.

PATHOLOGICAL

Urgent medical intervention required. High-risk situation needing immediate attention.
